Brush Class

Description

The inkbrush and octobrush are similar to rollers. But are weaker, and deal less damage, in exchange for speed. Inkbrushes are the faster of the two, but the inkbrush deals less damage per hit. The octobrush is a bit stronger. You can also roll just like rollers do, but won't one shot. Brushes are great for stealth, sneak up on your foes and get the kill!

Inkbrush Stats

  • Range: 10/100
  • Ink Speed: 100/100
  • Handling: 100/100

Octobrush Stats

  • Range: 25/100
  • Ink Speed: 80/100
  • Handling: 80/100
