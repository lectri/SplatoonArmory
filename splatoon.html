Weapon Classes

The different classes in the game.

Shooter Class

Description

The shooter is the default weapon in the game, it's most similar to an SMG. It has medium range, and medium damage. It can be good for attacking people, or for defending. Truly the jack of all trades. Just don't let weapons with high range/damage take advantage!

Flagship Stats

The Flagship for the shooters the splattershot. Here are it's stats.
Damage: 45/100
Range: 50/100
Fire Rate: 60/100

Charger Class

Description

The charger specializes in long range combat. Don't let your opponents sneak up on you! It's difficult to get kills from close up. The sniper has a laser that you and everyone else (including enemies can see. Try to hide your laser before sniping people.
Also, there are two variants of a sniper, scoped and unscoped. Unscoped has less range with more visibility, and scoped has the opposite.
You may also hold your charged shot, for a limited amount of time

Flagship Stats

The flagship sniper is the Splat Charger. Here are its stats.
Damage: 88/100
Charge Speed: 50/100
Mobility: 40/100

Roller Class

Description

The roller specializes in close range combat. It has three different methods of attacking.

  • Rolling, it's great for racking up turf, but good opponents can easily dodge the roll and kill you. Not good for combat.
  • Horizontal Flicking, it's good for attacking people up close and is easy to one-shot people with. Lacks range though.
  • Vertical Flicking. You can hit people from farther now, but it's difficult to aim far range.

Flagship Stats

The flagship roller is the Splat Roller. Here are its stats.
Range: 48
Ink Speed: 45/100
Handling: 55/100

Brush Class

Description

The inkbrush and octobrush are similar to rollers. But are weaker, and deal less damage, in exchange for speed. Inkbrushes are the faster of the two, but the inkbrush deals less damage per hit. The octobrush is a bit stronger. You can also roll just like rollers do, but won't one shot. Brushes are great for stealth, sneak up on your foes and get the kill!

Inkbrush Stats

  • Range: 10/100
  • Ink Speed: 100/100
  • Handling: 100/100

Octobrush Stats

  • Range: 25/100
  • Ink Speed: 80/100
  • Handling: 80/100

Dualies Class

Description

The dualies are essentially dual wield splattershots. They're quick but lack the range and damage of a single-wield. Also, you can roll with them, to dodge bullets friom your opponents!

Flagship Stats

  • Range: 50/100
  • Damage: 30/100
  • Mobility: 70/100
