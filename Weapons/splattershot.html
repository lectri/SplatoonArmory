Shooter Class

Description

The shooter is the default weapon in the game, it's most similar to an SMG. It has medium range, and medium damage. It can be good for attacking people, or for defending. Truly the jack of all trades. Just don't let weapons with high range/damage take advantage!

Flagship Stats

The Flagship for the shooters the splattershot. Here are its stats.

  • Damage: 45/100
  • Range: 50/100
  • Fire Rate: 60/100
